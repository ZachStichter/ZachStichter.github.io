Faith & Science

I believe that rigorous scientific inquiry and sincere religious faith are not only compatible but mutually enriching. This page is a space where I reflect on the interplay between my work as a scientist and my convictions as a person of faith.

"The same God who endowed us with sense, reason, and intellect has also intended for us to use them."
— Galileo Galilei
