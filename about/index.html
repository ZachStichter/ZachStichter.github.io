Zach Stichter

PhD Candidate · University of Notre Dame

I am a PhD candidate in the Chen Lab at the University of Notre Dame, where my research focuses on molecular dynamics of molecules under vibrational strong coupling. I investigate simulations of strongly coupled light-matter systems and apply machine learning to chemical problems. I am passionate about rigorous science and its intersection with broader questions of meaning and purpose.
